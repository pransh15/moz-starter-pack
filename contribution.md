# Contribute
- Thanks for your interest in contributing to Mozilla! The Mozilla Project welcomes contributions from everyone who shares our goals and wants to contribute in a healthy and constructive manner within our community. As such, we require all those who participate to agree to adhere to the Community Participation Guidelines in order to help us create a safe and positive community experience for all. 

## Quick Start:

### To make technical contribution:
* [Contribute to the Mozilla code base](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Mozilla/Developer_guide/Introduction)
* [Find your first code contribution](http://codetribute.mozilla.org/)
* [Contribute documentation to the Mozilla Developer Network](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/)
* [Contribute your design to an open design community](https://github.com/mozilla/OpenDesign/)
* [Using and Testing Nightly](https://activate.mozilla.community/en-US/nightly)
* [Build Your Own Extension for Firefox](https://activate.mozilla.community/en-US/webextensions)
* [WebVR Camp](https://activate.mozilla.community/en-US/webvr-camp)
* [Host a Technology Talk with Tech Speakers!](https://activate.mozilla.community/en-US/techspeakers)
* [Dive Into Rust](https://activate.mozilla.community/en-US/rust-hack)
